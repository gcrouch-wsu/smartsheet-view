How to update your program listing

You update your program’s public listing yourself. Open the link you were given (YOUR_VIEW_URL). You do not need a Smartsheet account—only the contributor login for this site.

Questions or problems: gradschool@wsu.edu

In the header or status area, use Contributor sign in.

First visit: choose First-time access, enter your @wsu.edu email, and create a contributor password (not your WSU password). You can register only if your email is already on your program row in the sheet behind this page.

Later visits: choose Sign in with the same email and contributor password.

After sign-in, find your program. Only rows where your email is authorized show Edit. Other programs may be visible but are not yours to edit.

Click Edit on your card. Change values only in fields that look like inputs or dropdowns; plain text without a box read-only. Use Save changes or Cancel. If you see a table layout, Edit may open a panel on the side—same steps.

Some pages show coordinators in grouped blocks. Use Add person / Remove when those buttons appear. Fixed slots may not allow adding people. Single contact columns may have Clear this role.

If something fails, read the error and try again. Still stuck? Email gradschool@wsu.edu with the page address, program name, time, and exact message.

Quick reference

Your @wsu.edu email must be in that row’s contact column to register. Password: 8+ characters, one uppercase, one number, one special character (e.g. ! * _). Password reset: email gradschool@wsu.edu.
