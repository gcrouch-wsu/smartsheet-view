# Contributor Instructions

Use this guide if you were sent a Smartsheet View page and need to update your own information.

## Who Can Create An Account

You can create a contributor account only if your `@wsu.edu` email address appears on the Smartsheet row in the configured contact field for that page.

That is the test:

- if your WSU email address is listed, you can create an account
- if it is not listed, you cannot create an account for that page
- if you think you should have access, contact `gradschool@wsu.edu`

## What You Need

- the page link you were sent
- your WSU email address
- a contributor password

Your contributor password is separate from your WSU password.

Password rule:

- at least 8 characters
- at least 1 uppercase letter
- at least 1 number
- at least 1 special character such as `!`, `*`, or `_`

## First-Time Access

1. Open the page link you were sent.
2. Select `Contributor sign in`.
3. Choose `First-time access`.
4. Enter your WSU email address.
5. Create your password.
6. Submit the form.

If your WSU email address is listed on the Smartsheet for that page, your account will be created and you can begin editing.

## Signing In Later

1. Open the same page.
2. Select `Sign in`.
3. Enter your WSU email address and contributor password.

## What You Can Edit

You can edit only the row or rows tied to your email address, and only the fields your administrator made editable.

## Editing Your Information

1. Sign in.
2. Find your row.
3. Click `Edit`.
4. Update the fields shown.
5. Click `Save changes`.

Some pages may also include grouped contact controls such as `Add person` or `Remove`.

## Password Reset

There is no self-service password reset.

If you cannot sign in, contact `gradschool@wsu.edu` and ask for a password reset link.

## Troubleshooting

### I cannot create an account

In most cases, your WSU email address is not listed on the Smartsheet row for that page. Contact `gradschool@wsu.edu` if you believe that is incorrect.

### I cannot see the Edit button

Check that:

- your WSU email is listed on the correct row
- contributor editing is enabled for that row or view
- the needed fields are marked editable

### My save failed

Try again after reading the error message. If it still fails, send `gradschool@wsu.edu`:

- the page URL
- the row you were editing
- the exact error message
- the time of the attempt

## Need Help?

Email `gradschool@wsu.edu` for help with contributor access, password reset, or editing problems.
